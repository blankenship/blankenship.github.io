Methodical Coffee

Methodical is a unique coffee bar located at the heart of downtown Greenville, SC in the newly-rennovated One City Plaza.

The shop features a hex-tiled mezzanine overlooking the bar, full glass front wall, marble & walnut surfaces, brass finishings, and an intimate outdoor patio, all in a compact footprint.

Our goal was the match the Methodical coffee methodology with an interior experience equal parts relaxed & sophisticated.

“Working alongside Ineō was such a joy! They helped focus our vision, and create a simple, humble atmosphere for coffee aficionados in Greenville.”—Will Shurtz, co-owner
